üîß Common Issues & Solutions

Setup

Guardian/Play Space Issues

Problem: Guardian keeps resetting or not appearing

Solution: Clear guardian history in settings, ensure good lighting, clean camera lenses, redraw boundaries in larger space

Performance

Lag or Stuttering

Problem: Games running poorly or choppy

Solution: Lower graphics settings, close background apps, ensure headset has 30%+ battery, restart headset, check for software updates

Tracking

Controller Tracking Lost

Problem: Controllers floating away or not tracking

Solution: Replace batteries, clean controller LED rings, improve room lighting, remove reflective surfaces, re-pair controllers

Comfort

Motion Sickness

Problem: Feeling nauseous in VR

Solution: Start with stationary experiences, take breaks every 20-30 min, use teleport movement (not smooth), increase framerate, ensure proper IPD

Connection

PC Link Not Working

Problem: Can't connect Quest to PC

Solution: Use official Link cable or high-quality USB 3.0 cable, enable developer mode, allow USB debugging, update GPU drivers, try Air Link

Audio

No Sound or Crackling Audio

Problem: Audio issues in headset

Solution: Check volume settings, disable/enable audio device in settings, restart headset, try different audio output (headphones vs speakers)
